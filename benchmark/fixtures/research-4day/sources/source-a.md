# Excerpt A — "Global Pilot Programme: Results Report" (4 Day Week Global, 2023)

61 UK companies (2,900 employees) completed the six-month pilot. Headline results: company revenue rose 1.4% on average over the trial (35 companies reporting); staff attrition fell 57% versus the prior six months; sick days fell 65%. 92% of participating companies chose to continue the four-day week after the trial. Employee self-reports: 71% lower burnout, 39% less stress.

Methodology notes (from the report's appendix): participation was voluntary and self-selected; companies chose their own implementation model; revenue and attrition figures are self-reported by participating companies; no control group was used. The publishing organization is an advocacy group whose stated mission is the adoption of the four-day week.
